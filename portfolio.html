homework 8 - data stories

To complete this task, I attended the Talking Economics conference.

I listened to two interesting panel discussions. One focussed on how inequality has risen during the Covid-19 pandemic, I was particularly interested to learn about the gender pension gap. A claim made during this talk was that on average women retire with pension pots 1/5 the size of men's. To create my fifteenth chart I scraped data from the PensionBee website in Google Colab which refutes this claim. I found that on average in the UK, women's pension pots are 62% that of men.
